A bumblebee blundered by Val's head on its way to visit the morning-glories. A bumblebee flew in the open window and buzzed dangerously near the hero's head. A tired bumblebee hums past, rolls lazily over a clover blossom at your feet, and has his midday luncheon. All at once, out of one of them, there flew a big buzzing bumblebee. Now and then a dove flashed by, and a golden bumblebee blundered into the chamber.
